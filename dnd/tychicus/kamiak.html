Kamiak

A City in Tychicus

Overview

Kamiak is one of the smallest walled cities in the country of Tychicus, but sure isn't the least important. The city is home to (among others) the Arcane Academy, where The country's best and brightest mages live, train, and study.

Arcane Academy

The Arcane Academy (often called the Arcane University) is where magical research is undertaken and where magical followers live and study.

St. Marindred's Theater in the Round

Built by Marindred in month 10, this ornate theater serves to host tragedies and comedies to entertain and educate the masses. It often collaborates with the nearby magic academy on sound amplification techniques and mystical melodies.
