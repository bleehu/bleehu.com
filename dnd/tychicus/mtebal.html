Mt. Ebal

A city in Tychicus

Overview

Mt. Ebal is both the name of the largest mountain in the range in central Tychicus and the city on its northern side. The city and mountain take their name from a biblical landmark, which means roughly Monument to God; Deliverer of Victory. Historically, the town Assisted moderately in the early bandit wars and rallied valiantly in the War of Establishment. In more modern times, The city holds a concealed ninja's dojo, which trains and houses many of Tychicus's covert operators and assassins.

Types of Bombs:

Oil pot:

Description: cheap to produce but not very powerful

Stats: 1 by 1 fire, +5 vs reflex, does ongoing 3 fire damage.

Cost: 1 gold for every 50

Molotov Cocktail:

Description: More powerful, but still very cheap

Stats: 3 by 3 fire, +8 vs reflex, does ongoing 5 fire damage

Cost: 1 gold for every 30 (it is assumed that you have three on you at the beginning of the adventure, all other bombs must be announced)

Bomb Modifiers:

Proximity Fuse:

Description: A useful but expensive tool, but it makes the bomb it is used on less powerful. Maybe with more research...

Stats: Used to set a bomb on a square when an enemy steps on said square the bomb will explode, stealth +4 to conceal the bomb from enemy sight, cannot be used with 1 by 1 or 2 by 2 bombs, reduces the size of a bomb explosion by two (3 by 3 becomes a 1 by 1 and so forth)

Cost: 5 gold per fuse

Timed Fuse:

Description:Timer functional for times up to 10 hours, but still very large and obvious

Stats: Used to set a bomb on a square. On the given time or turn, the warhead explodes. Detectable by either a dungeoneering, thievery, or perception check of a quarter your level unless otherwise camoflaged.

Cost: 3 gold per fuse
