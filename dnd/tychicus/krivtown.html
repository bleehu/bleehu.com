Krivtown

A small villiage in Northern Tychicus

Overview

After the fertile land to the Southeast of Kamiak was cleared of bandits, it was quickly settled by Tychian farmers and one of the more notable heroes of Tychian lore, after whom the town is named. The town has a small square with a modest farmer's market, and a burgeoning carpenter's business that specializes in ornate door construction and drywall repair.
