Cassalua

A Province in Tychicus

Overview

Cassalua is more of a large district than a single city. Cassalua joined Tychicus voluntarily just before the war with Necronia. During the Necrotic War, knights and soldiers from the kingdom of Cassalua aided greatly by defending Tychian towns.

Cassalua has roads to:

  • To Ramoth
  • To Kedesh
  • To Kamiak
  • To Mt. Ebal
  • To Krivtown
