Overview

During our time playing, some of the DM's have authored custom powers or custom equipment that our characters have found along the way. This page serves as a quick and easy place to come review what you have earned. Some of our older rewards are currently only in picture form, which you may view by scrolling or by right-clicking and selecting view picture from the drop-down menu. Please check back after completing adventures to see what you've unlocked!

Balasaar

Thrill of the Kill

At Will ♦ Immediate Reaction

Trigger: You reduce an enemy to zero HP or less.

Effect:You gain 3 adrenaline points and may place an assassin's shroud.

As your dagger plays accross your target's neck, your pulse quickens, your eyes dialate, your reflexs sharpen to a blurr...

Keyword: Adrenaline, kill skill, reaction

Effect: You gain 3 adrenaline points, and may place an assassin's shroud as a free action.

Note: All adrenaline points are lost during short or long rests. Adrenaline points may not be shared.

Assassin's Adrenal Riposte

At Will ♦ Immediate Reaction

Trigger: An enemy makes a melee attack against you.

Effect: You expend 2 adrenaline points, cancel the opponent's attack, and make a melee basic attack of your own.

The adrenaline coursing through your veins slows the world around to a crawl. As your enemy flourishes and stabs, you easily duck under his blade, and return the favor with lightning speed.

Keyword: Adrenaline, immediate reaction, riposte.

Requirement: You must have at least 2 adrenaline points.

Effect: The enemy's attack is cancelled (if it is an encounter or daily, the attack is considered expended). You make a melee basic attack against the enemy who attacked you. You lose 2 adrenaline points in doing so. If this attack reduces the target to 0 hp, You are refunded your 2 adrenaline points.

Milestone: If you have passed a milestone today, you may make a thievery check to disarm the opponent before you counterattack. A successful check must be higher than 10 + opponent's level + opponent's DEX mod.

As his allies turn to punish you for your violence, you disappear into the shadows, just in time to avoid their retaliation.

Effect: You gain an adrenaline point.

Note: All adrenaline points are lost during short or long rests. Adrenaline points may not be shared.

Adrenal Awareness

At Will ♦ Free Action

Trigger: You make a check to look for traps, creatures, items or topography.

Effect: Your senses are enhanced.

The adrenaline pumping through your ears and eyes heightens your senses to their limit. Each of your enemies heartbeats sound like thunderclaps. Each trap ahead of you is immediately apperent.

Keyword: Adrenaline, passive.

Effect: You gain enhanced senses as long as you have at least a certain level of adrenaline in your body.

3 adrenaline: You gain darkvision and a +2 to checks to find enemies.

5 adrenaline: You gain no-light vision, a +5 to checks to find enemies and a +10 to finding traps.

10 adrenaline: You gain no-light vision, and receive a +10 to checks for items, traps or topography. You can sense life through solid objects. You gain a +2 to reflex and AC.

Shadowsmoke

At Will ♦ Minor Action

Range: Burst 1 within 10 squares.

Effect: Creates a zone of Shadowsmoke.

You bend the shadows to your will, forming them into a thick, black smoke. You then launch it into the air, obscuring your foe's vision and making them cough and wheeze.

Keyword: Shadow, Arcane, implement.

Effect: You create a zone of Shadowsmoke.

Shadowsmoke: creatures other than you in the zone are slowed until the end of the turn that they entered it in, and receive a -10 to perception checks based on sight or smell.

Shadow Choke: If a creature ends its turn in the zone, it may immediately make a saving throw. If it fails, creature incurrs choking effects.

Related Feats

  • Acrid Smoke If a creature chokes, it also takes D4 poison damage
  • Fog of War Your attacks while in your smoke cloud get a +3 to hit. Attacks with the "shadow" keyword get an additional +2 to hit. Allies are not slowed by your shadowsmoke and do not choke.
  • Billowing Smoke Your zone inhibits an additional 5 to perception checks and enemies must roll 2 higher to successfully save against choking.

Fan The Cards

Encounter ♦ Stance

Duration: Until you move, or cancel the stance.

Effect: You cannot move from the square on which you stand, any enemy that attempts to force you to move must draw a card.

You knowingly pull 13 cards from the deck and fan them out, daring your foe to pick a card...

Keyword: Psionic, Stance, Cards, Impliment

Effect: You cannot move from the square on which you stand, any enemy that attempts to force you to move must draw a card. All forced movement is cancelled. This stance also allows the use of powers with the card keyword.

Drawing a card: Roll a D12, the number on the die is the number of the card drawn. A 1 is an ace. The King cannot be drawn.

Three: Slide Target three squares.

Four: Target takes ten damage from each of the following types: Fire, Cold, Shock and Force.

Six: Target takes six ongoing psionic damage.

Nine: Target takes 9d6 psionic damage.

Ten: Target heals 10 health.

Related Feats

  • Ace: Target is dominated. Save DC increases by 2 with every failure.
  • Five: Target is blind and immobilized for five turns. No chance to save.
  • Lucky Number Seven: Target is dominated for seven turns. No chance to save.
  • Eight: Target takes ongoing 8 psionic damage, for every 2 points of damage done, you heal 1. What the target can see, you can see.
  • Jack: You jack the target's gear. If you can equip either the target's weapon or armor, you may do so. That which you can't equip goes to your inventory.
  • Queen: Pick an ally that you can see. It heals 3d8 health.
  • Cutting the Deck: If an enemy makes a melee attack vs AC while you are in this stance, the enemy must pick a card.

Note: Cards will be awarded two per custom feat. Cards subject to change.

Choke Hold

At-Will ♦ Standard Action

Weapon: STR + Level / 3 VS REF.

Range: Touch.

Target: One Creature.

You silently sneak up behind the sentry. By grabbing him around his neck and mouth, you ensure that he can't cry out while his vision fades to black...

Attack: Strength vs Reflex

Keywords: Martial, Unarmed, Grab

Hit: Target is Marked, Immobilized and takes a -4 to attacks against you as long as the hold persists. Target must make saving throws vs choking. As long as the grab persists, target cannot make sounds as a free action.

Sustain Standard: Hold persists, and target must make another saving throw.

Choking: If target fails 2 throws, target is weakened. If target fails 3 throws, target is unconscious. If target fails 4 throws, target dies.

Related Feats

  • Jugular Press: Target automatically fails a saving throw on hit.
  • Spellstopping hold: As long as the hold persists, target cannot use powers that are spells, have the 'arcane' or 'voice' keywords.
  • Ghost Hold: You get +4 to stealth checks while the hold persists.
  • Body Shield: While the hold persists, You gain bonuses to your AC and REF equal to half that of the held enemy. If an enemy's attack misses you by less than your bonus, the attack hits your held target.
  • Woozy Walk: You get a +4 to checks to drag a target in this hold.

Note: an enemy knocked unconscious by choking doesn't lose any hit points, but will be helpless until any type of healing is received.

Note: Saving throws against choking will be made using the endurance skill, opposed by your athletics.
