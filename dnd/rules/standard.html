Standard Powers

There are a number of powers that every character has, as prescribed by the players manual. These are them.

Bull Rush

At Will ♦ Standard Action

Weapon: STR [W]

Melee: Weapon

Target: One Creature.

You push the enemy away from you.

Keyword: Martial.

Attack: Strength vs. Fortitude

Hit: You push the target 1 square. you may choose to shift into the square that they vacated.

Charge

At Will ♦ Standard Action

Weapon: STR [W]

Melee: Weapon

Target: One Creature.

you can launch yourself forward and make a melee basic attack.

Keyword: Martial, melee, basic.

Effect: Move your speed as part of the charge. At the end of your move, you make a melee basic attack with a +1 bonus to the attack roll. You must move at least 2 squares from your starting position, and you must charge to the nearest unoccupied square from which you can attack the enemy. Charging provokes opportunity attacks if you leave a square adjacent to an enemy during your movement. After a charge, you can’t take any further actions unless you spend an action point.

Grab

At Will ♦ Standard Action

Weapon: unarmed

Melee: Touch

Target: One Creature.

As a standard action, you can attempt to seize a creature adjacent to you and keep it from moving.

Attack: Strength vs Reflex.

Keyword: Martial, grab.

Requirement: You must have one hand free.

Hit: the enemy is immobilized until it escapes or you end the grab.

Sustain minor: the grab persists. You may end the grab as a free action.

Grab ends if: you are affected by a condition that prevents you from taking opportunity actions (such as dazed, stunned, surprised, or unconscious), you immediately let go of a grabbed enemy. If you move away from the creature you’re grabbing, you let go and the grab ends. If a pull, a push, or a slide moves you or the creature you’re grabbing so that it is no longer adjacent to you, the grab also ends.

Second Wind

Encounter ♦ Standard Action

Range: Personal

Target: yourself.

You get a second wind.

Effect: you can spend a healing surge to regain hit points. When you do, you regain hit points equal to your healing surge value. You also gain a +2 bonus to all defenses until the start of your next turn. You can use your second wind once per encounter.

Run

At Will ♦ Move Action

Range: Personal

Target: yourself.

You drop your defences and run as fast as you can.

Effect: You move your speed + 2 squares. You grant combat advantage and provoke opportunity attacks. You take a -5 to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.

Heal Other

At Will ♦ Standard Action

Range: Touch

Target: One Ally.

You use your skill in healing to aid your ally.

Effect: Make a DC 10 Heal check to allow an adjacent character to use his or her second wind without the character having to spend an action. The character doesn’t gain the defense bonuses normally granted by second wind.

First Aid

At Will ♦ Standard Action

Range: Touch

Target: One Ally.

You use your skill in healing to aid your ally.

Effect: Make a DC 15 Heal check. If you succeed, an adjacent ally can immediately make a saving throw, or the ally gets a +2 bonus to a saving throw at the end of his or her next turn.
