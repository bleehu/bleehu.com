About Us

Welcome to the Echo Team website! We are a group of UW students of different skill levels who enjoy playing Humans Vs. Zombie Tag at UW together!

ECHO stands for Elite Covert Hazardous Operations Team, and reflects our tendancy to opperate in zombie-infested areas.

Our site is currently in tactical developement, check back before and after Zombie Week! If there are any questions or comments regarding the website, ask Marmott or Wazowski. If there are any complaints, blame Wazowski.
